8 static HTML mockups for the Phase 1 Doctor View redesign. Open each in a browser, mark up changes, then we write the matching React code.

Premise

Dr. Rajesh Menon walks into CICU at 7 AM, taps the mic on the Briefing tab, asks “How does my day look?” — OmniSense reads back a 60-90 second narrative across his 11 cardiology patients, with priority ranking, overnight changes, and the questions waiting for him this morning. He follows up with “Tell me more about Ms. Fatima” → drill-down briefing with a patient confirmation step. Hands-free, fully cited, fully audited.

Conventions used in these mockups

  • All static HTML · one shared mockups.css · no build step, no React, no API.
  • Design tokens lifted verbatim from apps/ui/src/index.css (teal brand, IEC 62366 alert colours, Inter font, 48 px touch targets).
  • 5 worked patients deeply modeled · 6 stable patients show as one-line entries.
  • Single super-doctor Dr. Rajesh Menon (D001, CICU cardiologist) — demo simplification, real product is role-scoped.
  • Hand-drawn inline SVG body diagrams · clean line art · no licensing concerns · consistent with the rest of the UI.
  • Voice tab is the first tab — Briefing 🎤 · Rounds · Patients · Knowledge.
  • Every AI bubble carries a citation chip row · sources are mandatory.
  • Patient confirmation pills appear on every first mention in voice flows.
