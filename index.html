Aster Medcity demo — UI mockups

10 UI mockups for the OmniSense Clinical demo at Aster Medcity Kochi.

Premise

Dr. Rajesh Menon walks into CICU at 7 AM, taps the mic on the Briefing tab, asks “How does my day look?” — OmniSense reads back a 60-90 second narrative across his 11 cardiology patients, with priority ranking, overnight changes, and the questions waiting for him this morning. He follows up with “Tell me more about Ms. Fatima” → drill-down briefing with a patient confirmation step. Hands-free, fully cited, fully audited.

Voice doctor-only Push-to-talk Peer-consult tone Every reply cited Drug-safety pipeline PHI on-prem (production)
